The Underdog Story

From Columbia County to the World Stage

Chatham High School is a small rural school where 42% of students qualify for free-reduced lunch. We have declining enrollment and fewer than 90 students per graduating class. Most teams we compete against come from districts 5-10x our size with dedicated tech labs and corporate sponsors.

But here's what sets us apart: We refuse to charge students to participate. Every meal, every shirt, every travel expense — covered. Because talent shouldn't be limited by a family's bank account.

2024: The Championship Run

Last season, we won the Tech Valley Regional, earning our spot at the World Championship in Houston. At Worlds, we led our alliance to the highest-scoring match in our entire division and narrowly lost to the alliance that eventually won the division finals.

For a team of our size, from a district of our resources, that's not just success — that's validation.

Now we're building the next generation: we've established an FTC team at our middle school and a Lego League program at the Morris Memorial Center. Over 20 students are currently on our team, and we're growing.
